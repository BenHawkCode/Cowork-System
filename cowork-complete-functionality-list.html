Where Should You Start?

Follow these steps to get the fastest ROI from Cowork

1

Take the Cowork Readiness Assessment

Answer 40 questions about your current workflows. Get a personalised report showing exactly which features to prioritise based on where you are wasting the most time.

2

Start with your weakest area

Find the category where you scored lowest in the assessment. Focus on the Easy-rated use cases in that category first. Quick wins build momentum.

3

Connect 2-3 key integrations

Most business owners get the highest ROI from Gmail, Google Drive, and one CRM or financial tool (HubSpot or Stripe). Start there.

4

Set up your first scheduled automation

Pick one task you do every week manually. Set it up as a scheduled Cowork task. Once you see it running on autopilot, you will never go back.

5

Expand one category per week

Each week, add 2-3 new use cases from the next priority category. Within 30 days, you will have transformed how your business runs.
